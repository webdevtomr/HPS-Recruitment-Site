Shutdown & Projects Guarantees

HPS Recruitment – Shutdowns & Projects Guarantee

At HPS Recruitment, we know shutdowns and projects run on tight schedules with no room for error. That's why we guarantee a workforce that is skilled, compliant, and job-ready, every time.

Workforce Guarantee

We supply shutdown and project crews that meet all site-specific requirements, including:

  • Full compliance with inductions, tickets, and licences
  • Pre-screened & reference-checked candidates
  • Mobilised on time for project and shutdown deadlines
  • Fit-for-duty workers with all medicals and site requirements completed

Delivery Guarantee

We understand the critical nature of shutdowns and projects. Our guarantee is simple:

  • If a worker doesn't show or doesn't fit, we replace them fast — minimising downtime.
  • Dedicated account management ensures smooth coordination from recruitment through to mobilisation.
  • 24/7 support during shutdowns so you're never left short-staffed.

The HPS Advantage

  • Risk-free staffing – We take responsibility for payroll, compliance, and insurances
  • Peace of mind – We guarantee reliability so you can focus on execution, not staffing issues

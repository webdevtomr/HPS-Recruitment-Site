Labour Hire

What is Labour Hire?

Labour hire is when a private recruitment agency, like ours, employs workers and assigns them to your organisation. In this arrangement, you become the host employer, and we supply staff who are suited to your business needs.

How Labour Hire Works

Labour hire is when a business brings in workers through an agency instead of hiring them directly. The agency employs the workers, pays their wages, super, tax, and insurance, while the business (the host employer) manages them day to day. The host simply pays an agreed hourly rate to the agency, and everything else is taken care of.

What We Provide

We handle all employee obligations on your behalf, including:

  • Wages & PAYG withholding
  • Superannuation contributions
  • Payroll tax
  • Casual loading
  • Portable long service leave (if applicable)
  • Workers compensation & other required insurances

Our service fees are already built into the charge rate, meaning no hidden costs.

Why Use Labour Hire?

The host employer gains the benefit of skilled labour without the direct responsibility of payroll and compliance. We employ the worker directly, while you oversee their day-to-day duties as part of your team.

Locations We Service

We provide labour hire solutions across Perth (Joondalup, Welshpool, Osborne Park, Fremantle and beyond) as well as regional areas like the Pilbara.

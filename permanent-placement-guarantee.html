Permanent Placement Guarantees

HPS Recruitment – Permanent Placement Guarantee

At HPS Recruitment, we understand the importance of getting permanent hires right the first time. Our permanent placement guarantee gives you confidence, security, and a low-risk, flexible payment model when adding long-term staff to your business.

Placement Guarantee

Every candidate we recommend has been carefully sourced, screened, and interviewed to match your requirements. Our guarantee ensures:

  • Replacement Guarantee – If a candidate leaves within the agreed guarantee period, we will source a replacement at no additional cost.
  • Flexible Guarantee Periods – Terms tailored to the role and salary level, protecting your investment.

What We Promise

  • Thorough candidate screening – Skills, references, and compliance checked
  • Cultural fit assessment – Matching qualifications and team/company culture
  • Industry-specific expertise – Experienced candidates for operational roles, projects, and shutdowns
  • Transparent fees – Pay weekly for the work delivered, not a large upfront lump sum like most other agencies

The HPS Advantage

  • Low-risk hiring – You pay as you go and only for what you get
  • Save time & resources – We handle advertising, shortlisting, and interviewing
  • Quality over quantity – Only top candidates are presented for selection
  • Long-term partnerships – Supporting your business growth with the right people

The HPS Promise

With our permanent placement guarantee, you gain more than just a new employee — you gain peace of mind, flexibility, and a smart way to hire. Weekly payments mean you only pay for the value you receive, giving you control and transparency.

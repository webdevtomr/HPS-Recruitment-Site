Permanent Placement

What is Permanent Placement?

Permanent placement is our recruitment service for sourcing long-term employees who will be hired directly by your organisation. These roles often carry responsibility, leadership, or specialised skills within your business.

How Does it Work?

We take a tailored approach to permanent recruitment by:

  • Advertising and promoting your role
  • Screening and interviewing candidates
  • Presenting only the most suitable applicants for you to choose from

Unlike labour hire, these employees are hired directly by you and become part of your permanent workforce.

What We Provide

We do the groundwork — finding, screening, and shortlisting candidates — saving you time and ensuring you get quality applicants.

You, as the employer, take on full responsibility for wages, superannuation, payroll tax, leave entitlements, and any other statutory requirements once you hire the employee.

Fees & Guarantees

Our permanent placement service is charged as a percentage of the employee’s annual salary. We offer flexible payment options and provide guarantees for peace of mind. Salaries are always based on a minimum 38-hour work week over 52 weeks.

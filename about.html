About HPS Recruitment

At HPS Recruitment, we're driven by one simple principle: do right by people—clients and workers alike—and the results will follow.

We're a proudly Perth-based recruitment agency specialising in blue-collar labour hire and permanent placements. Founded by father and son team Ben and Harvey Heys, we bring hands-on industry experience and a deep understanding of what it takes to deliver real results on site.

Our Mission

Our mission is to set a new benchmark for blue-collar recruitment by providing fast, reliable, and honest workforce solutions—without inflated prices or empty promises. We exist to connect great companies with great workers, whether it's a one-off placement or a full site crew.

We take the stress out of recruitment for our clients and create real, ongoing opportunities for skilled tradespeople across Western Australia.

Our Experience

HPS Recruitment brings real, on-the-ground experience to every placement. Built by tradespeople, we understand the demands of WA worksites and deliver fast, reliable labour solutions without the red tape. From urgent same-day hires to long-term workforce planning, we move quickly, communicate clearly, and always prioritise quality over quantity.

  • Real-World Insight – We come from the tools, so we know what makes a quality worker and a productive site team.
  • Local Expertise – We understand WA worksites, compliance, and conditions because we've been on them.
  • Rapid Response – We've filled urgent same-day roles and built full project crews in under 48 hours.
  • Direct Access – With HPS, you deal with the owners—not call centres—so you get faster answers and real solutions.

Our Approach

We're hands-on, fast-moving, and personal. We take the time to understand your business and match workers not just by skill, but by attitude, reliability, and fit. For our candidates, we offer consistency, respect, and support, ensuring every placement is a positive step forward.

Why Choose HPS?

  • Trusted Partnerships – We focus on long-term relationships, not just filling shifts.
  • Right Fit, Every Time – Matching for skills, attitude, and reliability.
  • Responsive & Reliable – From one worker tomorrow to a full crew next week, we move fast.
  • Integrity First – Honest advice, transparent pricing, and zero fluff.

We don't aim to be the biggest agency in Perth—we aim to be the most trusted, dependable, and results-driven recruitment partner in the blue-collar space. We keep it simple: delivering quality people, fast—and backing it up with service clients remember.
